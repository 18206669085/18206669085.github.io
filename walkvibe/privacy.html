Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the WalkVibe：Gain From Every Step mobile application (“App” or “Service”). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only information necessary to operate, improve, and secure the Service.

  • Account Information: If you choose to sign in using Apple ID, Google, Facebook, or other supported services, we may receive your email address, display name, and profile picture for account management, reward tracking, and cross-device synchronization.
  • Fitness & Activity Data: With your permission, the App may access step count data, walking distance, activity duration, calorie estimates, and related motion information through Apple HealthKit or device motion sensors. This information is used solely to provide walking progress, activity rewards, and gameplay features.
  • Device & Technical Information: We may collect device model, operating system version, IP address, application version, language settings, time zone, screen resolution, network type, and advertising identifiers (IDFA) to improve compatibility, app stability, and fraud prevention.
  • Usage & Reward Information: We may collect activity history, daily streaks, reward claims, feature interactions, session duration, and ad engagement data to improve user experience and prevent abuse.
  • Analytics & Diagnostic Data: Crash logs, diagnostic reports, and performance information may be automatically collected to identify technical issues and improve Service reliability.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality.
  • Track walking activity and progress.
  • Calculate rewards, achievements, and milestones.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance.
  • Detect fraud, fake activity, or unauthorized behavior.
  • Deliver advertisements and promotional content.
  • Analyze activity trends and feature engagement.
  • Notify users about updates, rewards, and policy changes.

3. Health Data Usage

Any HealthKit or motion activity data accessed by the App is used exclusively for app functionality and is never sold to third parties.

Health-related data is not used for advertising purposes or shared with advertisers.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may only be shared in the following situations:

  • With trusted service providers supporting analytics, cloud storage, advertising, and app operations;
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process;
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the Service;
  • During mergers, acquisitions, or business transfers;
  • In anonymized or aggregated formats that cannot identify individual users.

5. Data Security

  • Encrypted data transmission using secure protocols;
  • Restricted access to sensitive information;
  • Routine security monitoring and maintenance;
  • Industry-standard protections against unauthorized access.

Although reasonable safeguards are implemented, no security system can guarantee complete protection.

6. Data Retention

  • Account information is retained while your account remains active.
  • Walking and reward data are retained to maintain progress and achievements.
  • Analytics and diagnostics may be retained for optimization and security purposes.
  • Information required by law may be retained where legally necessary.

7. Account & Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

  • In-App: Settings > Privacy > Delete Account

Please use the subject line: WalkVibe Account Deletion Request

Verified deletion requests are generally processed within 14 business days.
